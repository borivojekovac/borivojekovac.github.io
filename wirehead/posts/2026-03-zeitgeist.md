# Zeitgeist: Terminal Velocity

## Four frontier models in twenty-three days. Ninety-seven million agent installs. Thirty-eight thousand jobs dissolved. A video product killed by its own physics. Half a million lines of source code spilling across GitHub. March 2026 hits the speed where the turbulence becomes the signal.

The model releases arrive like ordnance on a schedule. [Grok 4.20 Beta 2](https://www.basenor.com/blogs/news/grok-4-20-is-live-whats-new-and-why-its-getting-faster "Basenor on Grok 4.20") on March 3, wired into X's firehose. [GPT-5.4 in three variants](https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/05/openai-launches-gpt-5-4-with-pro-and-thinking-versions/ "TechCrunch on GPT-5.4 launch") on March 5 - Standard, Thinking, Pro - a menu, not a model. [Mistral Small 4](https://mistral.ai/news/mistral-small-4 "Mistral AI on Small 4") on March 16, 119 billion parameters under Apache 2.0, running on a single GPU. [Gemini 3.1 Ultra](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/technology/ai/google-ai-updates-march-2026/ "Google on March AI updates") on March 20 with a two-million-token window digesting video natively. [Qwen3.5-Omni](https://www.marktechpost.com/2026/03/30/alibaba-qwen-team-releases-qwen3-5-omni-a-native-multimodal-model-for-text-audio-video-and-realtime-interaction/ "Marktechpost on Qwen3.5-Omni") on March 30, open-weight, aimed at Gemini's territory from Hangzhou. Capability compresses into commodity. The value migrates to the plumbing.

The plumbing had its own milestone. [MCP hit 97 million monthly SDK downloads](https://dev.to/alanwest/mcp-hit-97-million-installs-the-protocol-war-is-over-47ab "Dev.to on MCP milestone"), faster than Kubernetes ever scaled. [Google shipped a Workspace CLI](https://zenvanriel.com/ai-engineer-blog/google-workspace-cli-ai-agents-guide/ "Zen Van Riel on Google Workspace CLI") with native MCP - ten thousand GitHub stars in a week. At [GTC in San Jose](https://blogs.nvidia.com/blog/gtc-2026-news/ "NVIDIA blog on GTC 2026"), Jensen Huang framed the token as the unit of modern industry, [$1 trillion in demand](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/16/nvidia-gtc-2026-ceo-jensen-huang-keynote-blackwell-vera-rubin.html "CNBC on GTC keynote") through 2027, then unveiled the [Groq 3 LPU](https://www.breezyscroll.com/technology-news/nvidia-groq-3-lpu-gtc-2026-inference-chip/ "BreezyScroll on Groq 3 LPU") - not a GPU but a chip built from the [$20 billion Groq acquisition](https://www.datacenterdynamics.com/en/news/nvidia-builds-out-lpu-chip-team-following-20bn-groq-acquihire-announcement-rumored-for-gtc/ "Data Center Dynamics on Groq deal"), optimized for inference at 1,500 tokens per second. Agents now have silicon shaped to the speed at which they think. Five Fortune 500 companies [showed production agentic deployments](https://blogs.nvidia.com/blog/rtx-ai-garage-gtc-2026-nemoclaw/ "NVIDIA on NeMoCLAW and OpenCLAW") - 47-agent procurement pipelines, thousands of GPUs. The question at GTC was not viability. It was governance of systems already running.

The state is asking the same question, harder. After Washington’s failed attempt to block state AI regulation for a decade, the lines of conflict are drawn. The Pentagon [designates Anthropic a supply chain risk](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/05/anthropic-pentagon-ai-claude-iran.html "CNBC on Anthropic Pentagon designation") for refusing unrestricted military use of Claude. A federal judge [blocks it](https://www.cnn.com/2026/03/26/business/anthropic-pentagon-injunction-supply-chain-risk "CNN on Anthropic injunction") by month's end, but the leverage is registered. The [White House AI Framework](https://www.whitehouse.gov/releases/2026/03/president-donald-j-trump-unveils-national-ai-legislative-framework/ "White House on AI framework") lands March 20, preempting state laws. [Newsom signs his own executive order](https://www.reuters.com/sustainability/society-equity/california-ai-order-requires-firms-seeking-state-contracts-have-safeguards-2026-03-31/ "Reuters on Newsom AI executive order") ten days later. Federal preemption meets state defiance. The regulatory surface is contested ground now.

Then the fractures. [OpenAI killed Sora](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c3w3e467ewqo "BBC on Sora shutdown") on March 24, six months after launch, compute costs "economically irreconcilable." [Disney's $1 billion deal collapsed](https://arstechnica.com/ai/2026/03/the-end-of-sora-also-means-the-end-of-disneys-1-billion-openai-investment/ "Ars Technica on Disney deal collapse") the next day. Video generation at frontier quality requires physics that balance sheets cannot sustain. On March 31, [Claude Code's entire source](https://venturebeat.com/technology/claude-codes-source-code-appears-to-have-leaked-heres-what-we-know "VentureBeat on Claude Code leak") - 500,000 lines, $2.5 billion in annual revenue - [leaked through npm](https://alex000kim.com/posts/2026-03-31-claude-code-source-leak/ "Alex Kim's analysis of the Claude Code source leak"), revealing a "Self-Healing Memory" system and an "Undercover Mode" for stealth open-source contributions. That same day, [OpenAI closed $122 billion](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/31/openai-funding-round-ipo.html "CNBC on OpenAI $122B funding round") at $852 billion valuation. Between the two, [Claude Mythos leaked](https://fortune.com/2026/03/26/anthropic-says-testing-mythos-powerful-new-ai-model-after-data-leak-reveals-its-existence-step-change-in-capabilities/ "Fortune on Claude Mythos leak") - Anthropic's unreleased model, described in leaked documents as "far ahead of any other AI model in cyber capabilities." Agency leaking from the box in ways nobody budgeted for.

The human cost arrives in plain numbers. [Atlassian cut 1,600](https://www.reuters.com/technology/atlassian-lay-off-about-1600-people-pivot-ai-2026-03-11/ "Reuters on Atlassian layoffs") on March 11, [following Block's February purge of 40%](https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/12/atlassian-follows-blocks-footsteps-and-cuts-staff-in-the-name-of-ai/ "TechCrunch on Block and Atlassian layoffs"). Dell cut 11,000. [38,000 tech jobs gone in Q1](https://www.businessinsider.com/recent-company-layoffs-laying-off-workers-2026 "Business Insider layoff tracker"). [Anthropic's own study](https://fortune.com/2026/03/06/ai-job-losses-report-anthropic-research-great-recession-for-white-collar-workers/ "Fortune on Anthropic labor market study") found 75% of programming tasks within reach - the most exposed workers older, female, higher-paid. The people who thought expertise was armor. [BCG coined "AI brain fry"](https://hbr.org/2026/03/when-using-ai-leads-to-brain-fry "HBR on AI brain fry") for the cognitive overload of supervising machines. [Fink's annual letter](https://www.blackrock.com/corporate/investor-relations/larry-fink-annual-chairmans-letter "BlackRock CEO annual letter") named the shape: K. Leading firms surge, lagging firms spiral, the middle dissolves. [Morgan Stanley projects a 12-25% US power shortfall](https://fortune.com/2026/03/13/elon-musk-morgan-stanley-ai-leap-2026/ "Fortune on Morgan Stanley AI power warning") through 2028 - the physical ceiling on a digital ambition. The question from February - what will it cost to find out? - now arrives in human-shaped invoices. The bill is headcount.
